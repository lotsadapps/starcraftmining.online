Starcraft.Finance is a mining simulator and idle game on the blockchain. The more workers you have, the more Minerals they will generate (you mine at a rate of 1 Mineral per day). Buy more workers with your Minerals to multiply your production, or cash them out for BNB!

Automated Market

Mineral mining features a high tech automated market that lets you instantly buy workers or sell Minerals. Driven by supply and demand, the price automatically adjusts as players trade.

Referrals

Earn 10% the number of all Minerals used by anyone who starts playing using your link: http://starcraft.finance/?ref={Your Address}
